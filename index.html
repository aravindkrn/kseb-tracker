📖 About This App

KSEB TOD Consumption Tracker — estimates your bill per KSEB LT-1 Domestic (Single-phase) rules: slab/flat-rate billing, TOD multipliers, duty, fuel surcharge & fixed charges.

⚡ Tariff Classification Rules
  • Reviewed twice yearly: Jan–Feb and Jul–Aug
  • Avg monthly usage > 250 units → Non-Telescopic + TOD
  • Once Non-Telescopic, stays until next review
  • ≤ 250 units/month at review → back to Telescopic
📊 Telescopic Tariff (≤250 u/mo)
Slab-wise tiered billing. For bi-monthly billing, slab limits are doubled. TOD multipliers: T1 −10%, T2 +25%, T3 normal.
💡 Non-Telescopic Tariff (>250 u/mo)
Entire consumption billed at a single flat rate from non-telescopic table. TOD multipliers applied on usage shares.
🧮 Fixed Charges & Add-ons
  • Fixed Charges: slab-based per month; bi-monthly = 2× monthly FC
  • Electricity Duty = 10% of Energy Charges
  • Fuel Surcharge = 10 paise/unit
📅 Billing Due Date Logic
Always the 20th of the 2nd month after bill start. Example: Sep 20 start → Nov 20 due. Handles Feb and 31-day months automatically.
🕐 TOD Windows
  • T1 Normal: −10% discount (×0.90)
  • T2 Peak: +25% surcharge (×1.25)
  • T3 Off-peak: normal rate (×1.00)
